Bio

Hi! My name is Elliot and I am a Full-Stack Developer. I have a Front-End Nano-Degree from Udacity and am currently taking the University of Texas Full-Stack Developer Program. I decided to up my skills after I fell in love with coding two years ago.

While my background has not always been in web development, my passion for coding gave me no choice but to become a Full-Stack Developer. It also gives me an interesting perspective when it comes to my approach to my work, as I understand not only the development side of a project, but the business aspect too.

For much of my life I have lived between Texas and various countries in Europe. I love to travel and experience new cultures! This has really helped me in my new life in coding, as I am used to living outside of my comfort zone and always being pushed to learn and adapt.
