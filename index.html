Bio

Hi! My name is Elliot and I am a Full-Stack Developer. I have a Front-End Nano-Degree from Udacity and I am currently taking the University of Texas Full-Stack Developer Program. I have been coding for two years and have developed many front-end and full-stack projects.

In addition to web development, I also have experience in international business, which gives me an interesting perspective when it comes to my approach to my work. I understand not only the development side of a project, but the business aspect too.

For much of my life I have lived between Texas, France and Norway, with a detour via The Netherlands. I love to travel and experience new cultures! This has had a profound impact on my coding, as I am used to living outside of my comfort zone, always being pushed to learn and adapt while being well versed in communication.

Here are the technologies that I work with:

HTML • CSS • JavaScript • MERN Stack • MongoDB • Express.js • React • Node.js • SQL • PHP • Laravel • Handlebars • Firebase • Heroku • GIT • GitHub • API’s • Testing • SEO • Responsive Design

Cheers! -Elliot
